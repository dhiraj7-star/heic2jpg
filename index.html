What is HEIC and Why Convert to JPG?

HEIC stands for High Efficiency Image Container, a format introduced by Apple to store images more efficiently. Photos taken on iPhones and iPads running iOS 11 or later use the HEIF codec inside HEIC files. While this format delivers high quality at smaller sizes, it’s not supported by older systems, Windows, or many online apps.

That’s why users often need to convert HEIC to JPG — a universally compatible format recognized by all devices, browsers, and software like Photoshop, Windows Photos, and Android gallery apps. With HEIC2JPGs, you can instantly turn iPhone HEIC photos into standard JPGs without uploading files to any server.
