About

Our Mission

HEIC2JPGs was created with a simple but important mission: to provide a free, secure, and effortless solution for converting HEIC images to JPG format. We believe that technical compatibility issues shouldn't stand in the way of sharing precious memories and important images across different platforms and devices.

Why We Started

Like millions of iPhone users worldwide, we experienced the frustration of trying to open HEIC files on Windows computers, Android devices, and various online platforms. The existing solutions were either:

  • Complicated software that required installation
  • Online converters that uploaded our private photos to unknown servers
  • Paid services with hidden costs and limitations
  • Tools that compromised image quality during conversion

We knew there had to be a better way—a solution that respected user privacy while delivering excellent results. That's why we built HEIC2JPGs.

What Makes Us Different

Complete Privacy

Your images never leave your device. All conversion happens locally in your browser using advanced JavaScript technology.

100% Free

No hidden fees, no premium tiers, no watermarks. We believe essential tools should be accessible to everyone.

Instant Conversion

No registration required. Drag, drop, and download—your images are converted in seconds, not minutes.

High Quality

We preserve image quality during conversion, ensuring your photos look just as good in JPG format.

Our Technology

HEIC2JPGs leverages cutting-edge web technologies to deliver a seamless conversion experience:

  • Client-Side Processing: All image conversion happens directly in your browser using the powerful heic2any library
  • Modern Web Standards: Built with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript for optimal performance across all devices
  • Responsive Design: Works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
  • Security First: No server uploads mean no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access
